Hercules

It’s Summer. Look straight up. Do you see stars that look like a crooked H? You are looking at Hercules, one of the greatest heroes in the sky. The H is the body of Hercules. Other stars make up the hero’s head, arms and legs. He’s even holding a club.

Hercules was the strongest man who ever live. He became a god by performing twelve tasks, including battling a lion with his bare hands. That lion is now the constellation Leo the Lion.

The sky is full of other creatures. Hercules fought. He battles Hydra, the serpent with a hundred heads, ad Cancer the Crab. He hunted Cygnus the Swan and Aquila the Eagle. The great hero even fought and beat Draco the Dragon. Look up in the sky and you’ll see him there- standing over the defeated dragon.
