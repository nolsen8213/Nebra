Gemini, The Twins

Not far from Orion the Hunter are the Gemini Twins. The bright star Castor is the head of one twin. And the shining star Pollux is the head of the other.

In Greek myths, Castor and Pollux were the sons of Zeus. They were great warriors who sailed with Jason and the Argonauts aboard their ship the Argo. Once, Jason and his crew were trapped on an island with a giant who could kill his opponents with a single blow. The giant challenged the sailors of the Argo to a boxing match. But only Pollux was brave enough to accept the challenge. With skill and speed, Pollux defeated the giant, freeing the Argonauts to set sail again.
