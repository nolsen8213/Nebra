Sagittarius, The Archer

Sagittarius is a strange archer. He is half man and half horse, riding through the summer night sky. It’s no wonder he is holding a bow and arrow. To the Greeks, the archer is named Crotus. And it was Crotus who invented archery.

To most people, the eight bright stars of Sagittarius look more like a teapot with a handle, a lid and a top. There are also six stars close by that look like a teaspoon. Look closely and you’ll see other stars that look like a slice of lemon. You’re all ready for a tea party.
